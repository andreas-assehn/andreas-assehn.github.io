About Andreas Asséhn

Outside of work

Before writing software I was a carpenter. My dream of building my own house led me to an apprenticeship where I learned almost everything necessary to do just that. Like software, carpentry is all about solving problems and communicating with other professionals about optimal solutions. The freedom of not having a concrete solution to anything, because it all depends on the situation is what makes me intrigued and content with what I do.
