Latest build

Waggle

Waggle is a mobile view matching app to find playmates for your dog. Search from users in your area and filter based on distance. Once you match with another user you can chat with them and organize a meet or maybe attend an event? Events are also shown by distance and you can create your own. Our algorithm finds dogs similar in size, age, and energy level.

Technologies used include:

  • React
  • Socket.io
  • Express
